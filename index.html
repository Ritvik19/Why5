Fight back

Anonymous

Story begins with no words said, no deeds done,
Then how come the blames are blamed and I am the one
Feeling the wrath of the family, it’s anarchy,
Dealing daily with partiality, oh the patriarchy,
Growing up thinking you’re something special,
Crush the expectations put up to you like a wall,
Just like a warrior, yes I was a fighter in my mind,
Only to be reminded that I’m not one of their kind,
It needs more than just strength, the man muscles,
Wasn’t even given a chance to show the hustle,
I was deemed useless, pathetic, a loser in life
It wasn’t my choice, someone lost their daughter, someone their wife,
It was a show, to let everybody know,
that we’re glad, proud even to have someone like you,
But behind the locked doors of the house, I was still living in a sewage like a mouse,
Seen those tied up luggage on the end of a stick?
Seen those faces which feel like they were hit with a brick?
All this time thinking the love was for me and I could reach it
Only to realise it wasn’t meant to be cuz they were forced to do it
I used to be loved, adored, pampered and cared for
Who knew I’d be the one taking it granted for some bullshit reason that cannot be solved
So bad I wasn’t even thought of in the final decision far from involved,
And oh how they ended up looking down on me
It's just sad to see people are waiting to be disappointed in me
“Oh but tell me what did I do wrong to deserve this pain?”
“You don’t belong amongst us, understand? You ruined this white sheet of a thing like a stain.”
Constantly hearing things like that broke the confidence I had
Yet invisible for everyone to see how torn up I was or sad
Broke me down into pieces but fighting against me they were still
It's been said to me as if it's some prophecy I'm here to fulfil
No I’m done, I’m not here to do this anymore,
If you can’t accept me, leave that attitude out the door,
If this was right for me I wouldn’t feel so differently than what you claim I should be feeling and that I should be
kneeling to every single one of your “advice” but you know what? Kiss my ass goodbye.
